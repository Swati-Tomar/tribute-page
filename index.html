Albert Einstein

Timeline: The life and death of Albert Einstein
  • 1879 - Albert Einstein is born in Ulm, Germany, the son of Hermann Einstein, a German-Jewish featherbed salesman, and his wife Pauline.

  • 1884 - At the age of five, Albert Einstein becomes fascinated by his father's pocket compass, intrigued by invisible forces that cause the needle always to point north.

  • 1894 - Struggling financially, the Einstein family moves from Germany to Italy in search of better work. Albert, aged fifteen, stays behind in Munich to finish his schooling, but soon either quits or is kicked out of his high school and follows his parents to Italy.

  • 1895 - Albert Einstein attempts to get out of his last year of high school by taking an entrance exam to ETH,the Swiss Polytechnic University in Zurich. He fails the test,forcing him to attend one final year of high school in the small town of Aarau, Switzerland,instead.

  • 1896-Albert Einstein graduates from high school,begins attending ETH,the prestigious Swiss Polytechnic University in Zurich

  • 1901 -Albert Einstein obtains Swiss citizenship.

  • 1903 - Albert Einstein marries his longtime girlfriend, Milena Maric.

  • 1905 - Einstein submitted his first paper on his theory of thermodynamics. It was the first of many.

  • 1919 - After publishing his Theory of Relativity and correctly predicting the effects of a solar eclipse, Einstein's name went around the world.

  • 1922 - Einstein was awarded his first Nobel Prize. He was on his way to Japan when he got the news.

  • 1945 - World War II ended and Einstein continued his research. He made advances in heat, gravity, and relativity, and he published over 300 scientific reports.

  • 1955 - Albert Einstein died in April of heart failure.
